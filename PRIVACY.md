# Privacy Policy

**Short version: we don't collect anything. Nothing. Not a thing.**

That's not a typo. C2PAVerify gathers no data about you, your device, or how you use the app. We were going to write a long privacy policy full of words like "aggregate" and "third-party processors," but it turned out to be a very short document, because there's nothing to disclose.

## What we collect

Nothing.

- No analytics.
- No usage statistics.
- No crash telemetry.
- No advertising identifiers.
- No location.
- No contacts.
- No "anonymous diagnostics that are totally harmless, we promise."
- No accounts, no logins, no email lists.
- No cookies (it's an app, but still — none).

## What we send to servers

Nothing. The app verifies C2PA Content Credentials on your device. Your images and the files you check never leave your phone for our benefit, because we don't have a server waiting to hear from you.

## What we share with third parties

Nothing, because we have nothing to share. There is no data to sell, rent, lease, or "partner" over.

## What we store about you

Nothing. We don't know who you are, and we'd like to keep it that way — for both our sakes.

## Tracking

None. We don't track you across apps, websites, or in real life.

## Changes to this policy

If this ever changes — say, we add some feature that genuinely needs data — we'll update this document and make a lot of noise about exactly what changed and why. Until then, the policy is: nothing.

## Contact

Questions? Reach out to darkrockstudios@gmail.com. We probably won't have much to say about your data, on account of not having any.
